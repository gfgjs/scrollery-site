隐私承诺Privacy promise

「本地优先」不是营销词,是架构决定:数据留在设备上,是这款软件的默认物理形态。

“Local-first” is not a marketing word here; it is an architectural decision. Data staying on your device is this software's default physical shape.

应用:你的照片不出门

The app: your photos never leave

  • 照片、视频、文档与音频保持在你自己的磁盘上;Scrollery 原地索引,不上传任何媒体内容。
  • Your photos, videos, documents and audio stay on your own disks; Scrollery indexes them in place and uploads no media content.
  • 索引、缩略图与 AI 向量等派生数据同样存储在本机。
  • Derived data — the index, thumbnails, AI embeddings — is stored locally as well.
  • 语义搜索与人脸识别的模型推理全部在本机子进程中运行,断网可用;图像内容不发往任何服务器。
  • Semantic search and face recognition run entirely in a local subprocess and work offline; image content is sent to no server.
  • 没有账号体系,不要求注册。
  • There is no account system and nothing to sign up for.
  • 若你主动配置 WebDAV 网络存储(例如自家 NAS),库与缓存只流向你指定的服务器。
  • If you deliberately configure WebDAV storage (your own NAS, say), the library and caches flow only to servers you designate.

产品处于内测阶段;正式发布时将随安装包附带完整的隐私说明,若有任何联网行为(如检查更新),会在其中逐项列明并可关闭。

Scrollery is in private beta; the release build will ship with a full privacy statement enumerating any network activity (such as update checks), each item listed and switchable.

本网站:零 Cookie,零跟踪

This website: no cookies, no tracking

  • 不设 Cookie,不接入任何统计或分析脚本,不加载任何第三方资源。
  • No cookies, no analytics scripts, no third-party resources of any kind.
  • 你的主题与语言偏好保存在浏览器 localStorage 里,只存在于你的设备。
  • Your theme and language preferences live in your browser's localStorage, on your device only.
  • 本站托管于 Cloudflare Pages;Cloudflare 作为基础设施提供商,可能按其标准做法保留短期访问日志,我们不在其上叠加任何自有收集。
  • The site is hosted on Cloudflare Pages; as the infrastructure provider, Cloudflare may keep short-lived access logs per its standard practice. We add no collection of our own on top.
